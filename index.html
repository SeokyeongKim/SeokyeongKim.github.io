Seok-Young Kim

I am a researcher studying 3D Scene Generation and Human-AI Interaction in Mixed Reality. Currently an MS student at KAIST (GSMV), advised by Prof. Woontaek Woo.

My research investigates how spatial environments can be computationally parsed, reconstructed, and generated to support immersive experiences.
